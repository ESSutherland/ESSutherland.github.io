Welcome to Zanzibars

Zanzibars is a new local coffee house that roasts it's own coffee from the best coffee sources around the world. They also offer a range of sandwiches, salads and homemade soups; along with a wide variety of cakes, pastries and desserts. Local entertainment such as acoustic music will be provided on weekend evenings. The atmosphere is urban funk and located in the college-town center.
